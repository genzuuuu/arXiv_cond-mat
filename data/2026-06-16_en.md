### Topological Phases & Non-Hermitian Physics

*   **Complete Seven-Phase Anderson-Localization Landscape Realized:** An experiment in a 1D Floquet photonic lattice directly observed all seven predicted phases of Anderson localization, including the elusive triply coexisting extended-critical-localized state, establishing a complete experimental map of the transport hierarchy. ([#6](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14825))
*   **Topological Tricritical Ising Universality:** A study of a 1D cluster model reveals a topologically nontrivial tricritical Ising universality class, featuring a protected twofold degeneracy under open boundary conditions and an exponential energy splitting, contrasting with the algebraic splitting at the Ising* critical point. ([#47](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15588))
*   **Non-Hermitian Crystalline Braid Topology via Hermitian Projection:** A new mechanism is proposed where non-Hermitian topology emerges from projecting a fully Hermitian parent lattice onto an embedded subsystem. When the eliminated degrees of freedom host a zero mode, the resulting self-energy singularity generates complex band braiding and exceptional points. ([#247](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.06626))
*   **Anomalous Floquet Topology via Adiabatic Protocol:** An anomalous Floquet topological phase is realized experimentally using ultracold atoms in a hexagonal lattice by adiabatically modulating the lattice geometry, avoiding the heating issues of resonant Floquet driving. ([#129](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16942))
*   **Non-Abelian & Fracton Order (Toric Code/String-Net):** An analysis of long-range nonstabilizerness shows that mutual information can diagnose the obstruction to removing "magic" from topologically encoded states with shallow circuits, fully classifying it for the toric code but not for doubled Fibonacci string-net models. ([#272](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.22424))
*   **Non-Hermitian Bulk-Boundary Correspondence:** A tensor-network framework is developed to compute real-space spectral functions for 3D non-Hermitian topological insulators with billions of lattice sites, enabling direct observation of corner modes in the macroscopic regime. ([#100](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16424))

### Strongly Correlated Systems & Magnetism

*   **Spin-Orbit Coupling & Fluctuations:**
    *   **Dynamic Jahn-Teller Effect in $5d^2$ Systems:** Coupled-cluster calculations on Cs$_2$WCl$_6$ show that the dynamic Jahn-Teller effect modulates RIXS spectra and magnetic moments, occurring in a weak regime without noticeable static deformation. ([#53](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15697))
    *   **Spin-Orbit Entangled Magnetism in Bond-Disordered Pyrochlore:** $\mu$SR and NMR studies of NaCdCo$_2$F$_7$ reveal a spin-glass-like state with ongoing dynamics from spin-orbit-entangled $J_{\rm eff}=1/2$ Co$^{2+}$ moments, a behavior fundamentally different from its $S=1/2$ Cu counterpart and driven by spin-orbit coupling. ([#124](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16875))
    *   **Magnetic Control of Polariton Condensate:** Magnetically tunable exciton-polariton condensation is demonstrated in the van der Waals magnet CrSBr, with the condensate energy tunable by up to 10.5 meV in a 2 Tesla field. ([#181](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.06010))
    *   **Hidden Chirality & Half-Vortex Formation:** A radially symmetric nonresonantly pumped polariton condensate is shown to acquire chirality without a rotating drive, leading to all-optical formation of spin-selective half-vortices. ([#83](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16233))
*   **Pseudo- and Higher-Spin Systems:**
    *   **Staggered Loop Current Order in Dice Lattice:** Short-range interactions in a pseudospin-1 Dirac flat band are shown to drive a staggered virtual-loop-current order, leading to time-reversal symmetry breaking and anomalous Hall conductivity. ([#76](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16155))
*   **Quantum Spin Liquids & Frustration:**
    *   **Strange Metal Spin Fluctuations in Overdoped Cuprate:** NMR data on La$_{2-x}$Sr$_x$CuO$_4$ at $x=0.25$ shows low-energy spin fluctuations suggestive of quantum criticality persisting well beyond the spin-stripe critical doping, with spatial inhomogeneity likely resolving the paradox. ([#232](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13573))
    *   **Itinerant SDW Order in Ruddlesden-Popper Nickelates:** A theoretical model shows that magnetism in La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$ and La$_4$Ni$_3$O$_{10}$ is fundamentally itinerant, driven by mirror-selective interband nesting, providing a unified explanation for observed spin-wave-like spectra. ([#237](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.20148))
*   **Twisted Moiré Systems:**
    *   **Valley Domain Walls in Rhombohedral Graphene:** Valley domain walls in rhombohedral graphene act as impenetrable transport barriers in the metallic state, requiring intervalley interactions for transmission. In the superconducting state, such interactions are crucial for Josephson supercurrents through SNS' junctions. ([#12](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14878))
    *   **Chern Number Switching in Twisted MoTe2:** Interlayer sliding and gating are shown to switch the valley Chern number in alternately twisted tetralayer MoTe$_2$, providing a new way to engineer moiré band topologies. ([#44](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15548))
    *   **Moiré Trapping of Quadrupolar Excitons:** Atomistic calculations reveal that quadrupolar excitons in twisted WSe$_2$/WS$_2$/WSe$_2$ trilayers are trapped at moiré lattice sites, with different in-plane symmetries. This trapping is critical for realizing frustrated quantum magnetism in these systems. ([#107](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16557))
    *   **Valley Polarization in WSe2 via Kagome Oxide:** A van der Waals heterostructure of WSe$_2$ with the ferromagnetic kagome oxide V$_2$O$_3$ is predicted to achieve a robust valley splitting of ~10.4 meV at zero magnetic field via the magnetic proximity effect. ([#52](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15666))
    *   **Superconducting Spin Valve in vdW Trilayer:** A gate-tunable superconducting spin valve effect is predicted in a van der Waals F/S/F trilayer, allowing continuous switching between different spin-valve regimes and exotic non-BCS superconducting states. ([#194](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.09093))
    *   **Chiral Topological Superconductivity in Twisted Graphene:** A theoretical investigation shows that chiral $p_x+ip_y$ pairing in twisted bilayer and double bilayer graphene leads to tunable topological superconducting phases with distinct Chern numbers. ([#198](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.17380))

### Superconductivity

*   **Bilayer Nickelate Superconductors:** A review article discusses the interlayer pairing mechanism for superconductivity in Ruddlesden-Popper bilayer nickelates, emphasizing the hybridized $d_{x^2-y^2}$ and $d_{z^2}$ electronic structure and strong interlayer magnetic coupling. ([#32](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15298))
*   **Flat-Band Superconductors & Vortex Core:** In a flat-band superconductor on the Mielke checkerboard lattice, the vortex-core size is shown to be governed by the quantum metric of the Bloch states, diverging at band edges, qualitatively different from the BCS picture. ([#105](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16536))
*   **Geometric Supercurrent of Fluctuations:** A study shows that superconducting fluctuation transport (paraconductivity) provides a direct probe of quantum information geometry. The longitudinal paraconductivity is governed by the quantum Fisher information of Cooper pairs, while the anomalous Hall effect is governed by the mean Uhlmann curvature. ([#63](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15928))
*   **Superconducting Diode Effect via Floquet Engineering:** A study theoretically demonstrates the superconducting diode effect in a driven Rashba nanowire, where the drive amplitude controls the switching between Floquet Majorana 0- and $\pi$-modes and the diode efficiency. ([#102](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16459))
*   **Plasmons in Low-Valence Nickelates:** Resonant inelastic x-ray scattering (RIXS) was used to detect plasmons in Pr$_4$Ni$_3$O$_8$ for the first time. They are more damped than in cuprates and soften with temperature, revealing a distinct charge-screening landscape. ([#200](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.12160))
*   **Correlated Superconductivity in Twisted Bilayer Graphene:** A variational Gutzwiller study reveals a strongly correlated superconducting phase in magic-angle twisted bilayer graphene at large $U$, distinct from a conventional Mott insulator and a weakly coupled BCS phase. ([#224](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.04631))
*   **Magnetic Breakdown and QO in Kagome Metals:** Theoretical work shows that slight changes in hybridization gaps in kagome metals can strongly reshape quantum oscillation phases via magnetic breakdown, masking the nontrivial Berry phase of an isolated orbit. ([#215](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.05816))
*   **Fulde-Ferrell-Larkin-Ovchinnikov (FFLO) Phase:** A unified classification of superconducting orders, the "Berezinskii-Abrahams hypercube", is presented, organizing known phases (e.g., BCS, $p$-wave, FFLO, PDW) and predicting new hybrid orders like odd-frequency FFLO states. ([#183](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.10079))

### 2D Materials & van der Waals Heterostructures

*   **Ferroelectricity via Confined Oxygen Vacancies:** The robust ferroelectric switching in ultrathin Bi$_2$SeO$_5$ is shown to originate from confined oxygen-vacancy migration, where vacancies undergo reversible rearrangements but long-range diffusion is suppressed. ([#66](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16006))
*   **Quadrupolar Exciton Imaging:** The internal structure of quadrupolar excitons in twisted WSe$_2$/WS$_2$/WSe$_2$ trilayers is revealed at an atomic scale. Their real-space localization at moiré lattice sites is critical for future many-exciton phases. ([#107](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16557))
*   **Phonon Confinement in hBN:** Helium-3 spin-echo spectroscopy directly resolves the intrinsic linewidths of low-energy phonons in few-layer hBN, revealing a strong confinement-induced reduction in phonon lifetimes for interlayer shear modes. ([#60](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.15818))
*   **Defect Tolerance in Trigonal Se Photovoltaics:** First-principles calculations show that trigonal selenium is intrinsically defect-tolerant, as Shockley-Read-Hall recombination via point defects is strongly suppressed, despite the presence of deep levels. ([#251](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.11403))
*   **Valley-Valve Transistor Action:** Gate-controlled multipath dynamics of fractional quantum Hall edge excitations are imaged with ~100 ps resolution, revealing the controllability of edge magnetoplasmon trajectories. ([#223](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.29234))
*   **Anisotropic Charge Transport in p-n Heterojunction:** Anisotropic band dispersion in GeS is shown to drive directional charge flow in a GeS/MoS$_2$ heterostructure diode, enabling orientation-dependent diode characteristics and optoelectronic response. ([#174](https://arxiv.org/abs/2405.14817))

*   **Hidden Chirality & Half-Vortex Formation:** A radially symmetric nonresonantly pumped polariton condensate is shown to acquire chirality without a rotating drive, leading to all-optical formation of spin-selective half-vortices. ([#83](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.16233))
