## Superconductivity

**New Superconducting Phase in Infinite-Layer Nickelates**
- [A Disconnected Superconducting Regime at the Parent Limit of Infinite-Layer Nickelates](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16684): Pristine PrNiO₂ exhibits intrinsic superconductivity without cation substitution—a regime separated from the conventional doping dome, suggesting distinct physics from cuprates.
- [Marginal-Fermi-Liquid-like Behavior without Pseudogap in Infinite-Layer Nickelates](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16852): ARPES reveals momentum-dependent marginal-Fermi-liquid scattering in nickelates but no detectable pseudogap, clarifying what's essential for high-Tc superconductivity.
- [Unconventional superconductivity in ScIr₂ chiral crystal with a kagome lattice](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16689): Two-gap superconductivity with nodal structure in a chiral kagome material, linking flat bands and strong correlations.
- [Tuning superconducting pairing symmetry via a staggered potential in the doped honeycomb Hubbard model](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16663): Staggered potential controls the transition between d+id- and f-wave pairing—a band-engineering tool for artificial graphene superconductors.
- [Symmetry-Based Microscopic Theory of the Unconventional Pairing Mechanism in La₅Ni₃O₁₁](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18094): Two-gap pairing in hybrid nickelates, with interlayer d₂² and intralayer dx²-y² channels explaining the reduced Tc compared to La₃Ni₂O₇.

**Josephson and Proximity Effects**
- [Eddy currents and current reversal in curved magnetic thin-film Josephson junctions](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16399): Curvature controls supercurrent distribution and enables current reversal in proximity-coupled ferromagnets.
- [Observation of Critical Current Minimum in Super-Honeycomb Josephson Junction Arrays](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16500): Critical current dips at f=1 in super-honeycomb arrays indicate long-range inter-junction hybridization.
- [Field-free Josephson diode with controllable efficiency using magnetic tunnel barrier](https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.04650): Zero-field diode effect with ~40% efficiency in NbN/GdN/NbN junctions, tunable via micromagnetic structure.
- [Nonreciprocal Superconducting Transport from Chiral Edge States](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18159): Boundary-controlled chiral edge states produce Josephson diode effect in kagome antiferromagnets.
- [A Voltage-Controlled Josephson Frequency Comb](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.17992): Gate-tunable superconductor-semiconductor junction generates coherent microwave frequency combs.

**Spectroscopic Probes**
- [Superconducting Acoustogalvanic Effect in Twisted Transition Metal Dichalcogenides](https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.21436): Surface acoustic waves as a novel probe of pairing symmetry in van der Waals superconductors.
- [Quantum-geometric thermal conductivity of superconductors](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.11608): Quantum metric governs thermal Meissner stiffness, establishing Wiedemann-Franz-type bounds.
- [Visualization of vortex sheets and half quantum vortices in UPt₃](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.03908): SQUID microscopy reveals half-quantum vortices, confirming chiral odd-parity superconductivity.

## Topological Phases and Quantum Matter

**Altermagnetism**
- [J₁-J₂-δ model on a square lattice: From Altermagnet to Columnar antiferromagnet](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16415): Quantum fluctuations stabilize a quantum disordered phase between altermagnetic and columnar AFM states.
- [Electrical Control of Altermagnetism in a Quasi-1D Magnet](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16856): Electric field linearly controls d-wave spin splitting in AgCrP₂S₆.
- [Stripe-Order Altermagnetism](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.17997): Stripe-order altermagnet with mirror-selected nematic spin splitting—distinct from rotation-based l-wave classification.
- [Quantum-metric-driven light-induced ferrovalley state in d-wave altermagnets](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.17049): Pure quantum-metric effect drives orbital-selective valley polarization, measurable via ARPES.
- [Spin-phonon interaction in a symmetry-enforced spin-polarized state](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.17980): Raman spectroscopy reveals symmetry-dependent spin-phonon coupling in altermagnet CoNb₄Se₈.

**Floquet and Driven Systems**
- [Stroboscopic stability of a Floquet chiral spin liquid](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16515): Topological doublet survives far beyond the folding frequency; stability governed by local energy scales.
- [Dissipative Stabilization of Floquet-Engineered Many-Body Order](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16391): Coupled auxiliaries cool system toward Floquet-engineered ground states, stabilizing discrete time crystals.
- [Time-Crystalline Phase in a Single-Band Holographic Superconductor](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.13252): Subharmonic growth indicates broken time-translation symmetry in holographic model.
- [Discrete time crystal order in spin chains enabled by Floquet flat bands](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.14307): Two-tone flat-band segment suppresses thermalization, stabilizing period-doubled response.

**Quantum Hall and Related**
- [Thermal and viscous contrast in quantum Hall scanning-probe images](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16315): Framework for interpreting scanning-probe images; thermoelectric contrast pinned at E_c=μ.
- [Colored ΔT noise probes the topological character of edge modes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.17354): Finite-frequency ΔT noise distinguishes chiral, helical, and trivial edge modes.
- [Layer-engineered quantum anomalous Hall effect in twisted rhombohedral graphene](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.14014): Chern number programmably set by layer number; in-situ switching between C=3 and C=4.
