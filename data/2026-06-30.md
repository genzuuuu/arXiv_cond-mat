### Superconductivity & Strongly Correlated Systems

- **Nickelate Superconductivity:** A perspective paper reviews the "Spin-Fermion-Hubbard Model" as a successful theory for high-Tc superconductivity in cuprates, contrasting it with the more common t-J model approach. [Theory of High-Tc Superconductivity in Cuprates](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28906) A debate on the pairing mechanism in La₃Ni₂O₇ uses ARPES data to argue that a Hund's rule-driven mechanism involving d_{x²-y²} orbitals is most relevant, as other models predict gap nodes not seen experimentally. [What Does the Single-Particle Spectrum Imply on the Pairing Nature and Pairing Mechanism in La$_3$Ni$_2$O$_7$?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29470) A unified theory reconciles the role of quantum geometry, band dispersion, and correlations in determining whether ferromagnetism or antiferromagnetism wins in narrow-band systems. [Ferromagnetism vs. Antiferromagnetism in Narrow-Band Systems: Competition Between Quantum Geometry and Band Dispersion](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.03575)

- **Unconventional Superconductors:** The chiral topological semimetal Ag₂Pd₃S is found to be a superconductor (Tc=1.1K) with spontaneously broken time-reversal symmetry, hosting Kramers-Weyl and other multifold fermions. [Unconventional Superconductivity in the Chiral Topological Semimetal Ag2Pd3S](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29767) Strong evidence for pair density waves (PDW) in the kagome superconductor CsV₃Sb₅ is provided via STM, showing 2x2 pairing modulations that are highly sensitive to non-magnetic impurities. [Phase-sensitive evidence for pair density waves in a kagome superconductor](https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.10958)

- **Heavy Fermions & Kondo Physics:** Magnetotransport in UTe₂ up to 42T reveals that the field-induced SC3 phase appears below the metamagnetic transition, though zero resistance is only reached above it. [Unusual upper critical field in UTe2 revealed by magnetotransport measurements up to 42 T](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28904) A study on the Kondo lattice CeSn₀.₇₅Sb₂ shows a possible inverse magnetic melting effect, where an antiferromagnetic phase is melted by an applied magnetic field. [Possible inverse magnetic melting effect in vdW-like Kondo lattice CeSn$_{0.75}$Sb$_2$](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.02820)

- **Superconductivity Mechanisms:** A review reframes the jellium model for superconductivity, finding Tc is only fractions of a Kelvin in metallic hydrogen due to weak coupling. [Superconductivity in Jellium Model Revisited](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29306) An experimental comment clarifies that a linearized phonon Boltzmann equation cannot produce a true two-terminal thermal rectification ratio. [Comment on "Fundamental limit of phonon Tesla valve for heat rectification from first principles"](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28407)

### Topological Phases & Quantum Geometry

- **New Topological Orders & Indices:** A new 16-fold family of fermionic topological orders is identified, distinguished by a mod 16 't Hooft anomaly of a Z₂ one-form symmetry. [Sixteen-Fold Way for Fermionic Topological Orders](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28682) A "Position Space Chern Number" is introduced to classify chiral magnetic systems, with the new index demonstrated in skyrmion phases. [The Position Space Chern Number: A Topological Index for Chiral Magnetic Systems](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28549)

- **Floquet & Non-Hermitian Topology:** A study shows that periodic driving can generate strong, second-order, and hybrid-order topological insulators in a single four-band system with spin-orbit coupling. [Floquet topological phases with time-reversal and space inversion symmetries and dynamical detection of topological charges](https://arxiv.org/abs/2406.08532) A PT-symmetric quantum impurity model is solved, showing monotonic impurity entropy flow despite non-unitary interactions. [Monotonic Impurity Entropy beyond Unitarity: the $\mathscr{PT}-$Symmetric Quantum Impurity Model](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28495)

- **Quantum Geometry & Transport:** A gauge-consistent theory shows that length and velocity gauges give the same intrinsic nonlinear dc conductivity, resolving a conceptual ambiguity. [Length--Velocity Gauge Equivalence of Quantum Geometric Nonlinear Conductivity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29663) Hall viscosity in quantum Hall systems can be probed via circular dichroism using a rotating quadrupolar drive. [Hall viscosity from metric-sensitive dichroic probes](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.30051) The concept of "quantum metric protection" is introduced, where a band's quantum metric pins the localization length at a finite value even with increasing disorder. [Quantum Metric Localization and Quantum Metric Protection](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.03987)

[Propagation and localization of spin excitations at altermagnetic domain walls](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.10604)

- **Ultrafast Dynamics & Spintronics:** Strain engineering in the vdW ferromagnet Fe₃GaTe₂ is shown to continuously tune both the coercive field and accelerate ultrafast demagnetization. [Bayesian model comparison of type-I and type-II ultrafast demagnetization dynamics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.30334)

- **Skyrmions & Transport Phenomena:** Repulsive skyrmions in structured chambers exhibit asymmetric diffusion through a gate, with transient "bond" formation. [Diffusion asymmetry of repulsive skyrmions in structured environment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.14090) The topological Hall effect is observed in the centrosymmetric antiferromagnet EuAuAs, attributed to field-induced spin textures. [Field-induced topological Hall effect and butterfly-shaped magnetoresistance in the centrosymmetric antiferromagnet EuAuAs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29813)
