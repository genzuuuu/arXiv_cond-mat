# Today's arXiv Papers Summary (Robotics & AI)

## Embodied Intelligence & Manipulation Policies

**[HiMoE-VLA: Hierarchical Mixture-of-Experts for Generalist Vision-Language-Action Policies](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.05693)**
Addresses negative transfer in VLA policies trained on heterogeneous robot data. Uses hierarchical MoE to specialize computation for different action spaces and embodiments. Achieves 3.98 on CALVIN, 98.0% on LIBERO, demonstrating that negative transfer can be turned into positive transfer with proper architecture.

**[FabriVLA: A Lightweight Vision-Language-Action Model for Precise Multi-Task Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08575)**
A compact VLA combining InternVL3.5 with flow-matching action head and gated self-attention. Achieves 90.0% on Meta-World MT50 with only 1B parameters, showing that multi-billion parameter backbones aren't strictly necessary.

**[GeoProp: Grounding Robot State in Vision for Generalist Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07101)**
Lightweight adapter that aligns proprioception with vision through geometric grounding and spatial feature sampling. Projects robot state onto image plane, improving Diffusion Policy by 8.7% and pi_0 by 4.0% across 67 tasks while adding only 2-3% parameters.

**[Pelican-VLA 0.5: Attending Before Acting Benefits Generalization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06655)**
Unified VLA with future-frame generation and action prediction. Uses learnable Bottleneck Token between perception and action that enables attention-level generalization to unseen scenes/embodiments without task-specific fine-tuning.

**[TFP: Temporally Conditioned Memory-Fusion Policies for Visuomotor Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08283)**
Adds Liquid Time-Constant memory dynamics to VLA backbones for stage-dependent manipulation. Improves LIBERO from 96.9% to 98.75% and achieves 75% on memory-focused MIKASA ShellGameTouch. Mechanistic analysis shows belief causally modulates action chunks.

**[V-VLAPS: Value-Guided Planning for Vision-Language-Action Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.00969)**
Augments VLA-guided planning with lightweight value head predicting Monte Carlo returns. On LIBERO, improves by +6pp on Object and +4pp on LIBERO-10 with larger search budget, showing VLA representations support value-guided planning.

**[SeFA-Policy: Fast and Accurate Visuomotor Policy Learning with Selective Flow Alignment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.08583)**
Selective flow alignment strategy correcting generated actions to match observations while preserving multimodality. Reduces inference latency by over 98% compared to diffusion/flow baselines while maintaining accuracy.

**[SkillPlug: Unsupervised Skill Mining for Few-Shot Adaptation in Robotic Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08354)**
Plug-in framework mining shared skill library from multi-task demonstrations via self-supervised objectives. Keeps skills fixed for adaptation by fine-tuning only lightweight router and action head for new tasks, improving data efficiency.

**[Harness VLA: Steering Frozen VLAs into Reliable Manipulation Primitives via Memory-Guided Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08448)**
Memory-augmented agentic framework exposing frozen VLA as retryable contact-rich primitive. Composes with analytic primitives, improving over baselines by 38.6pp on LIBERO-Pro and 25.4pp on RoboCasa365 without finetuning the VLA.

**[Play2Perfect: What Matters in Dexterous Play Pretraining for Precise Assembly?](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.26428)**
RL framework for task-agnostic play pretraining then finetuning for precise assembly. Shows 33x sample efficiency over from-scratch RL. Achieves 60% zero-shot sim-to-real on tight insertions (0.5mm clearance) and 50% on multi-part assembly.

**[EgoWAM: World Action Models Beyond Pixels with In-the-Wild Egocentric Human Data](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08436)**
Studies what world representation best enables human-to-robot transfer. Finds DINO improves OOD generalization by 4x and 3D flow improves in-domain performance by 20-30% compared to pixel-based prediction, demonstrating scalable human data supervision.

**[WAM-TTT: Steering World-Action Models by Watching Human Play at Test Time](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06988)**
Test-time training that steers frozen world action models from raw human videos via self-supervised video prediction. Achieves efficient steering without robot actions, annotations, or fine-tuning, outperforming in-context human-video conditioning.

**[VOTE: Vision-Language-Action Optimization with Trajectory Ensemble Voting](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.05116)**
Finetunes VLA models for fewer action tokens with high parallelism. Introduces voting-based ensemble strategy combining current/previous action predictions, achieving 39x faster inference than OpenVLA with 46Hz throughput on edge platforms.

## Dexterous Manipulation & Hands

**[DexVerse: A Modular Benchmark for Multi-Task, Multi-Embodiment Dexterous Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08751)**
Large-scale benchmark with 100 tasks spanning grasping, articulated objects, tool use, bimanual coordination. Supports 3 arms and 6 dexterous hands with configurable visual variations. Benchmarks Diffusion Policy, DP3, OpenVLA, pi_0 across 19 tasks, revealing substantial challenges.

**[AnyDexRT: Calibration-Free Dexterous Hand Retargeting with Few-Shot Human Guidance](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08341)**
Calibration-free retargeting combining self-supervised fingertip correspondence with few-shot human guidance. Refines pinch poses using contact classifier. Shows improved retargeting quality across diverse dexterous hands without precise calibration.

**[TouchWorld: A Predictive and Reactive Tactile Foundation Model for Dexterous Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07287)**
Hierarchical policy separating vision-language planning, tactile world-model prediction, and high-frequency tactile residual refinement. Achieves 65.0% success clean, 53.7% under perturbations, outperforming strongest baseline by 15.7 and 18.7 pp. Uses touch as both predictive reference and fast feedback.

**[BiNoMaP: Learning Category-Level Bimanual Non-Prehensile Manipulation Primitives](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.21256)**
Three-stage RL-free framework for bimanual non-prehensile skills (pushing, pivoting). Extracts hand trajectories from egocentric video, optimizes into executable primitives, parameterizes by object size. Supports cross-embodiment transfer on different dual-arm platforms.

**[ContactMimic: Humanoid Object Interaction via Contact Control](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08742)**
Learning framework tracking explicit part-level binary contact commands alongside keypoint trajectories for humanoid object interaction. Achieves precise physical contact and controllability across 10 diverse motions, validated in sim and real.

Unified grasping framework for both soft and rigid objects using only RGB input. Combines open-vocabulary detection, boundary-aware point assignment, and language-based stiffness estimation. Validated on Franka arm with lettuce, cheese, croissants, bottles.

## Teleoperation & Sim-to-Real

**[SPEAR: A Simulator for Photorealistic Embodied AI Research](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06701)**
Python library controlling any Unreal Engine application with 14K programmable functions. Renders 1920x1080 at 73 FPS (10x faster than existing UE plugins). Provides non-diffuse intrinsic image decomposition, material IDs, and supports co-simulation with MuJoCo.

**[EVIS: A Physics-Grounded Event Camera Plugin for NVIDIA Isaac Sim](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.08098)**
Faithful log-intensity contrast event model with per-pixel asynchronous updates for Isaac Sim. Offers interpolation via bidirectional motion-vector warping for real-time generation. Generates high-rate, fully labeled event streams for downstream tasks.

**[Rapidly Learning Soft Robot Control via Implicit Time-Stepping](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.06667)**
Uses DisMech implicit simulator with delta natural curvature control for soft robot learning. Parallel stepping of 500 environments achieves up to 40x faster speeds for contact-rich scenarios vs Elastica. Demonstrates sim-to-sim transfer.

**[A Continual Learning Framework for Adaptive Control of Modular Soft Robots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06740)**
Continual learning framework for MSR control that adapts to morphology changes without forgetting. Validated on pneumatic soft robotic arm. Enables module-specific dynamics learning for localized control.

## Robot Locomotion & Navigation

**[Behavior Foundations for Quadruped Robots: ABot-C0 Technical Report](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07370)**
Generalist motion-control for quadrupeds with multi-source data pipeline (16K motion clips). First demonstration of scaling law for quadruped motion tracking via Flow-Matching policy. Three-stage privileged-to-perceptive framework with temporal LiDAR memory.

**[Physics-Guided Biomechanical Gait Adaptation for Humanoid Locomotion](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07830)**
Two-stage framework for humanoid locomotion on steep slopes. Slope-adaptive ZMP regularizer + Biomechanical Slope Gait Adapter. Achieves blind traversal of outdoor slopes up to 62.7% (32.1°) in sim-to-real experiments.

**[HumAIN: Human-Aware Implicit Social Robot Navigation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.07357)**
Fuses implicit social cues (skeletal keypoints, gait) into planning via knowledge distillation. Transformer teacher distills into lightweight student, improving trajectory prediction by 29.8% over SOTA baselines on resource-constrained platforms.

**[LHM-Humanoid: Long-Horizon Human Motion Control for Continuous Object Transport](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.16943)**
Reset-free long-horizon motion for simulated humanoid: walk, lift, carry, place objects repeatedly. Uses goal-conditioned controller with learned release-and-retreat behavior for smooth transitions. Outperforms end-to-end RL across 350 cluttered layouts.

**[GemNav: Discrete-Token Visual Robot Navigation using a Multimodal LLM](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06882)**
Adapts frozen MLLM for waypoint navigation via LoRA on language tower only. Uses single discrete token vocabulary for waypoints and navigation signals. Trained on 8.7 hours of data, zero-shot transfers to 4 unseen environments (0.25-0.42m accuracy).
