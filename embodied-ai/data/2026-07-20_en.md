# Today's Robotics & AI Papers Summary

## Vision-Language-Action Models & Manipulation Policies

**[Xiaomi-Robotics-1: Scaling Vision-Language-Action Models with over 100K Hours of Real-World Trajectories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15330)**
Xiaomi presents a foundational VLA model trained on 100K+ hours of real-world manipulation data collected via UMI devices. A two-stage recipe (pre-training + post-training) enables out-of-the-box mobile manipulation in unseen environments and efficient fine-tuning. Achieves 57.6% on RoboCasa365 and 20.07 on RoboDojo, establishing new SOTA.

**[RhinoVLA Technical Report](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15330?utm_source=chatgpt.com#)** *(Note: same URL as above, likely different paper)*
A deployment-oriented VLA model co-designed with edge SoC, achieving 11.69 Hz inference using token-efficient backbone and hardware-aware compilation.

**[GigaWorld-Policy-0.5: A Faster and Stronger WAM](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13960)**
Action-centered World Action Model that uses future visual dynamics during training but action-only decoding at inference. Mixture-of-Transformers architecture achieves 85 ms latency on RTX 4090.

**[AC-VLA: Robust OOD Action Execution via Compositional Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15714)**
Addresses OOD generalization failures in VLA models through compositional learning with LLM-driven instruction decomposition and asymmetric masking. Achieves ~28% improvement on LIBERO-OOD.

**[MindDrive: VLA for Autonomous Driving via Online RL](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.13636)**
Combines LLM reasoning with online RL by using discrete linguistic decisions as action space. Achieves 78.04 DS on Bench2Drive with lightweight Qwen-0.5B.

## Dexterous Manipulation & Robotic Hands

**[VTAP Gripper: Synergizing Fingertip Sensing and a Visuo-Tactile Active Palm](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15448)**
A tactile-reactive gripper combining an actuated visuo-tactile palm with compliant fingers. Demonstrates in-hand syringe reorientation, peg-in-hole, and singulation of 3mm objects. Proposes staged gesture-conditioned retargeting for teleoperation.

**[Handroid: Bridging Dexterous Hand and Humanoid](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16187)**
A desktop-scale reconfigurable robot (2.05 kg, 27 DoF) that transforms between a dexterous anthropomorphic hand (20 DoF) and a bipedal humanoid. Validated on grasping, in-hand manipulation, locomotion, and embodiment reconfiguration tasks.

**[Continuously Stable Structure through Plastic Deformation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15659)**
Soft gripper using plastic deformation for passive holding force up to 16 N without energy input. Stable under 400 m/s² acceleration, enables passive perching for mobile robotics.

## Tactile Sensing & Contact Perception

**[MuxGel: Simultaneous Dual-Modal Visuo-Tactile Sensing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.09761)**
Spatially multiplexed sensor using checkerboard pattern to capture both external vision and tactile signals through a single camera. U-Net reconstruction from sim-to-real pipeline enables plug-and-play GelSight integration.

**[Towards Artificial Nerves: Biomimetic Optical-Fiber Tactile Sensing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15746)**
OptiTac sensor emulates mechanoreceptor-to-nerve architecture using optical fibers as artificial nerves. Simple analytical methods (no deep learning) infer contact location, size, and shape.

**[VTLoc: Tactile Contact Localization in Visual Point Clouds](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16146)**
Framework localizing tactile contact points on 3D point clouds using geometric multi-modal alignment and iterative refinement. Evaluated on 100 real-world objects.

**[Scalable Open-Source Visuotactile Sensor for 6-Axis Wrench Estimation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15633)**
Visuotactile endcap sensor for tensegrity robots. Gyroid-infill bonding technique for durable elastomer-TPU interface. Neural network maps shear vector fields to 6D force/torque.

**[ContactFusion: Stochastic Poisson Surface Maps from Visual and Contact Sensing](https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.16592)**
Fuses point clouds with force/torque contact sensing into Stochastic Poisson Surface Maps. Demonstrates improved peg-in-hole pose estimation.

**[BayesContact: Uncertain Pose Estimation via Visuo-Tactile Proposals](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16123)**
Simulation-based inference framework for peg-in-hole pose estimation. Maintains particle belief over object pose, fusing depth with force/torque contact evidence. Improves insertion success over vision-only by 30%.

## Teleoperation & Data Collection

**[Let the Body Follow: Coupled Egocentric Control for Whole-Body Teleoperation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.16095)**
Body-following teleoperation where robot torso/base automatically respond to operator's head/arm motions. Evaluated on TIAGo mobile manipulator for home-care tasks, reducing button-based control effort and mental demand.

**[Worlds in One Demo: Synthetic Data Engine for Mobile Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13154)**
WANDA generates diverse training data from a single real demonstration by reconstructing Gaussian splats and robot-object trajectories, then rearranging contact-rich segments into new spatial configurations.

**[Data and Learning Where it Matters for Contact-Rich Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15982)**
Automated data collection with offline RL for critical contact-rich task segments only, using traditional planning for free-space motion. Achieves 96% success across 4 tasks with only 2-2.5 hours of autonomous data collection.

## Sim-to-Real & Robot Learning

**[SLAC: Safe Real-Robot RL via Unsupervised Simulation Pre-Training](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.04147)**
Learns task-agnostic latent action space from low-fidelity simulator for safe real-world RL. Learns contact-rich whole-body tasks in under 1 hour of real interactions without demonstrations.

**[Sym2Real: Symbolic Dynamics with Residual Learning for Adaptation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.15412)**
Data-efficient controller adaptation using symbolic regression on noise-free simulation data, then residual adaptation with ~10 real-world trajectories. Validated on quadrotor and racecar.

**[NeuralActuator: Neural Actuation Modeling for Robot Dynamics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.11734)**
Jointly predicts torque surrogate, external forces, and motor condition for low-cost servo platforms. Improves behavior cloning when used as pretrained module.

**[Difference-Based Relational Learning for Zero-Shot Object-Goal Navigation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15642)**
T-DRN combines Siamese difference-based features with temporal buffer for domain-independent representations. Validated on physical wheeled robot with direct sim-to-real transfer.

## Learning-Based Manipulation & Planning

**[EmbodiedDiffusion: End-to-End Traversability-Guided Visual Diffusion](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.02851)**
Diffusion-based framework predicting traversability maps and generating trajectories from RGB images. FiLM-based conditioning enables cross-platform transfer with 10 min data collection.

**[Dynamics-Aware Meta-Imitation for Generalization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15880)**
DAMI framework integrates meta-learning with Visual-Motor Trajectory module for capturing spatio-temporal dynamics. Outperforms baselines on seen and unseen tasks via few-shot fine-tuning.

**[IMBench: Benchmark for Intuitive Robotic Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15641)**
35-task benchmark evaluating integrated physical reasoning and execution. Reveals VLA models struggle to satisfy task constraints, identifying intuitive manipulation as missing capability.

**[RAVEN: RL-Adaptive Visibility-Graph Planning for Humanoid Navigation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.15701)**
Hierarchical RL-MPC framework where RL adapts geometric construction of visibility-graph planner to compensate for delays and tracking imperfections in humanoid navigation.
