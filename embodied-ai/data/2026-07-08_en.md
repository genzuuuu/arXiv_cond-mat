# Today's arXiv Papers: Robotics & AI Highlights

## 📌 Dexterous Manipulation & Multi-Finger Grasping

**LAMP: Latent Motion Prior-Guided Real-World Learning for Dexterous Hand Manipulation**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06323)
Introduces a latent motion prior module that maps hand-action histories to compact latent codes, enabling efficient real-world dexterous learning. Combines imitation learning with online RL, achieving 56.25% average IL success rate and 98.75% after RL fine-tuning across four tasks with real robot hands.

**HANDFUL: Sequential Grasp-Conditioned Dexterous Manipulation with Resource Awareness**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.25126)
A learning framework for sequential dexterous manipulation where finger usage is modeled as a limited resource. Introduces finger-level contact rewards to encourage resource-aware grasps for downstream tasks like pushing, pulling, and pressing while maintaining initial grasp control.

**Do Robots Really Need Anthropomorphic Hands?**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.05415)
Systematic review of 125 papers (2019-2025) comparing human vs robotic hands. Finds that in-hand manipulation doesn't benefit from anthropomorphic design—simpler mechanisms suffice. However, mechanism complexity correlates with task breadth. Argues for focusing on robustness and softness over replicating human hand anatomy.

**GraspIT: A Dataset Bridging Sim-to-Real for Validated Grasping**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05869)
Large-scale grasping dataset (~2.3M candidates) with physical slip-test validation in Isaac Sim. Features 1035 simulated and 100 real scenes with 6-DoF grasp annotations, providing hard negatives from force-closure-passing but slip-test-failing grasps.

## 📌 Teleoperation & Motion Retargeting

**DexTele: A Dual-Arm Dexterous Teleoperation System**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05883)
Combines vision-based motion retargeting with adaptive force control. Uses motion-graph encoder and latent optimization for precise cross-platform retargeting, plus VLM-MPC integration for compliant grasping force prediction.

**HumanoidUMI: Robot-Free Demonstrations for Humanoid Manipulation**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.27239)
Extends UMI to humanoid whole-body data collection using lightweight VR devices. Collects sparse keypoint trajectories and wrist views to train high-level policies for coordinated perception, locomotion, and manipulation.

**RynnWorld-Teleop: Digital Teleoperation via World Models**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06558)
Replaces physical robots with generative world models for data collection. Operator's hand-pose stream drives a Diffusion Transformer to synthesize egocentric videos from single reference images. Achieves 40+ FPS interactive generation and zero-shot Sim2Real transfer.

**WristMimic: Full-Body Humanoid Control for Manipulation**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06438)
Separates contact-free body tracking from contact-rich hand manipulation. Uses wrist as the natural gate between kinematic pose targets and learned grasping behaviors, enabling finger-agnostic retargeting across diverse hand embodiments.

## 📌 Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models

**Lift3D-VLA: Lifting VLA Models to 3D Geometry and Dynamics**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06564)
Unified VLA framework with explicit 3D point cloud reasoning. Uses Geometry-Centric Masked Autoencoding for joint 3D structure and dynamics learning. Achieves 10.8% higher success on MetaWorld and 11.1% on RLBench over prior VLA methods.

**PoseVLA: Universal Pose Pretraining for VLA Policies**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.19710)
Decouples VLA training into spatial pretraining (discrete pose tokens in camera-centric space) and embodiment alignment. Achieves 79.5% success on RoboTwin 2.0 and 96.0% on LIBERO, with robust real-world generalization from 100 demos per task.

**SIEVE: Structure-Aware Data Selection for VLA Imitation Learning**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06442)
Discovers reusable visuo-motor primitives and transitions in demonstrations, then selects medoid trajectories within composition patterns. Surpasses full-data training using only 50% of demos and 50% of training steps.

**ActionCache: Training-Free Acceleration for VLA Models**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06370)
Plug-and-play external cache that reuses intermediate actions from flow-matching VLA models. Achieves 11.75× acceleration for π₀.5 and 34.43× for GR00T-N1.6 while maintaining task success rates.

**ResTacVLA: Residual Tactile Representation for VLA Models**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.03387)
Reformulates tactile data as residual representation capturing discrepancy between visual priors and physical sensations. Uses VQ bottleneck for Latent Contact Primitives to capture visually-missed critical events, preventing modality collapse.

## 📌 Visuomotor Policies & Imitation Learning

**RoboTALES: Task-Aligned Simulated Futures for Robot Policies**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06018)
Uses hierarchical LLM-based planner to break tasks into subgoals, then guides video generation with a VLM-based critic. Improves long-horizon manipulation on RoboCasa and LIBERO10 through temporally consistent imagined rollouts.

**FORGE: Functional Tool-Use Generalization via Keypoint Reasoning**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05780)
Two-stage policy decoupling functional reasoning from action execution. Uses keypoint trajectories as intermediate representation for functional generalization—achieves over 2× improvement on unseen tools for hitting tasks.

**Choose What to Observe: Task-Aware Semantic-Geometric Representations**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.07875)
Canonicalizes visual input into shared representation using SAM3 segmentation and Depth Anything for OOD robustness. Improves policy performance under appearance shifts without modifying or fine-tuning the policy itself.

**SCOReD: Student-Aware CoT Optimization for Recommendation Distillation**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05734)
Optimizes chain-of-thought traces for recommendation by pruning redundant sections while preserving information-dense parts using student LLM attention scoring. Reduces reasoning length by 27.3% while improving NDCG by 1.56%.

## 📌 Sim-to-Real & World Models

**EgoDyn-Bench: Evaluating Ego-Motion in Vision-Centric Foundation Models**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.22851)
Diagnostic benchmark revealing a "Perception Bottleneck" where VLMs have logical physical concepts but fail to align them with visual observations. Providing explicit trajectory encodings restores physical consistency.

**Image2Sim: Scaling Embodied Navigation via Generative Neural Simulator**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05765)
Constructs 3D feature-Gaussian environments from posed RGB-D sequences, generating nearly 20K interactive scenes and 10M+ navigation training samples. Transfers effectively to real-world zero-shot settings.

**First Plan Then Evaluate: Multi-Target Grasping Evaluation**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.07162)
Framework planning trajectories to multiple grasp candidates, then evaluating success likelihood at terminal configurations before execution. Improves over standard generator-evaluator-planner pipeline across objects and environments.

**WAM4D: Fast 4D World Action Model via Spatial Register Tokens**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.14048)
Uses lightweight spatial register tokens for training-time future-depth readouts to transfer geometric priors into a causal video-action transformer. Improves spatial consistency in manipulation while maintaining efficient inference.

**RoboDojo: Unified Sim-and-Real Benchmark for Generalist Policies**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.04434)
42 simulation and 18 real-world tasks evaluating generalization, memory, precision, long-horizon execution, and open-vocabulary following. Integrates 30 policies with standardized evaluation protocols.

## 📌 Robot Learning & Control

**Learning to Throw Objects Safely in Multi-Obstacle Environments**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06388)
Introduces potential field state representation encoding basket attraction and obstacle repulsion for RL-based throwing. Achieves up to 90% success in cluttered real-world scenes with robust sim-to-real transfer.

**Optimal Transport Q-Learning for Flow Policy Steering**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06262)
Fine-tunes flow-based policies using advantage-weighted conditional optimal transport flow matching. Increases success from 36% to 86% for single-task policies and 38% to 76% for pretrained VLA, while reducing inference steps by 70%.

**Actuator Reality Shaping for Zero-Shot Sim-to-Real**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02205)
Shapes physical actuator behavior to match idealized simulation dynamics, enabling zero-shot policy deployment without task-level fine-tuning. Validated on 7-DOF arm, wheeled-legged, and humanoid robots.

**Hypothesis-driven Model Expansion for Open-World Robot Planning**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06501)
Framework enabling robots to generate, verify, and update hypotheses about abstract world models using foundation models. Integrates hypothesis verification into goal-reaching planning for unknown environments.

## 📌 Embodied Reasoning & Navigation

**EAGOR: Embodied Reasoning in Omni-Direction with Spherical Harmonics**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06165)
Training-free geometry-aware framework using Spherical Harmonic Belief Fields for 360° directional reasoning. Achieves +34.4% and +45.6% gains on HOS and OSR-Bench, improving navigation success by 14.6%.

**GEM-Occ: From Visual Geometry to Embodied Semantic Occupancy Memory**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.05543)
Gaussian Evidence Memory framework for semantic occupancy mapping. Treats visual geometry predictions as transient evidence, fusing into persistent hierarchical memory through visibility-aware updates. Supports building-level mapping.

**UniLM-Nav: Unified Framework for Zero-Shot Last-Mile Navigation**
[Link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.06537)
Decomposes last-mile navigation into view selection, affordance grounding, and base-pose reasoning using shared MLLM backend. Outperforms prior SOTA on OVMM benchmark by 3.13 percentage points.

## 🏠 Summary

Today's papers show a strong trend toward **integrating 3D geometric reasoning into VLA models** (Lift3D-VLA, WAM4D, PoseVLA), with several works addressing **data efficiency** through clever selection (SIEVE), caching (ActionCache), and simulation-to-real scaling (GraspIT, RoboDojo). **Tactile sensing for VLA** emerges as a key direction (ResTacVLA), while **dexterous manipulation benchmarks** (HANDFUL) and **humanoid whole-body control** (WristMimic, HumanoidUMI) continue to advance. The sim-to-real gap is being tackled from both directions—shaping real actuators to match simulation (Actuator Reality Shaping) and building better generative simulators (Image2Sim, RynnWorld-Teleop).
