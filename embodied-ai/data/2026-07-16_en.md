# Today's Robotics and AI Papers Summary

## Dexterous Manipulation & Industrial Benchmarks

**[Industrial Dexterity Benchmark: A Hardware-Software Benchmarking Platform for Industrial Dexterous Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14021)**
Introduces IDB boards mimicking datacenter cable management and assembly tasks, plus a scalable imitation learning framework (DAG-ROS) and multimodal diffusion policy (AG-iDP3) fusing RGB, point clouds, joint positions, and wrench data. Best configuration (multi-view RGB + R3M encoder) achieves 78% grasp-and-insert success on cable manipulation.

**[Where to Touch, How to Contact: A Hierarchical RL-MPC Framework for Geometry-Aware Sim-to-Real Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2601.10930)**
Hierarchical framework where a high-level RL policy predicts contact intentions (touch location + subgoal pose), and low-level contact-implicit MPC generates real-time actions. Achieves zero-shot sim-to-real on pushing, pivoting, and reorientation with 10x less data than end-to-end baselines.

**[Learning Forward & Reverse Skills from a Single Unfinished Demonstration for Constrained Manipulation Tasks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13882)**
One-shot framework learning both forward and reverse execution from a single, possibly incomplete demonstration. Decomposes into non-contact (DMP) and contact phases (screw motion primitives with admittance control). Demonstrates peg insertion, battery insertion, lock opening.

## Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models

**[Generalizable VLA Finetuning via Representation Anchoring and Language-Action Alignment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13429)**
Anchor-Align prevents representation drift during BC finetuning via frozen VLM distillation and language-action alignment. Improves real-robot success from 28%→54% and 37%→60% on xArm7 across two architectures.

**[Semantic Anchoring for Robotic Action Representations](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13597)**
Shows action representations degrade during VLA finetuning. Proposes anchoring to a semantic manifold with shared/private channels. Yields +18.7% on real-world in-distribution and +21.5% on OOD tasks.

**[Move-Then-Operate: Behavioral Phasing for Human-Like Robotic Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.23620)**
Explicitly decouples manipulation into coarse relocation and contact-critical interaction phases via dual-expert policy with learnable phase selector. Achieves 68.9% vs 44.9% on RoboTwin2, matching 10x more data baselines.

**[Beyond Description: Cognitively Benchmarking Fine-Grained Action for Embodied Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.18685)**
CFG-Bench: 1,368 videos with 19,562 QA pairs evaluating physical interaction, temporal-causal reasoning, intentional understanding, and evaluative judgment for embodied agents.

**[When Vision Overrides Language: Evaluating and Mitigating Counterfactual Failures in VLAs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.17659)**
Introduces LIBERO-CF benchmark for counterfactual failures where VLAs ignore language and act on visual shortcuts. Proposes Counterfactual Action Guidance (CAG) that compares VLA with vision-only policy. Improves π0.5 by 9.7% in language following.

## Tactile Sensing & Multimodal Fusion

**[Tactile Modality Fusion for Vision-Language-Action Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.14604)**
TacFiLM uses Feature-wise Linear Modulation (FiLM) to condition visual features on tactile representations. Improves success rate, completion time, and force stability on insertion and drawer opening tasks.

**[Design, Modeling and Experimental Validation of a Miniature Hybrid Underwater Glider](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13622)**
FoDeGlider with foldable deflectable wings for confined underwater navigation. Multibody dynamics model treats wing configuration as structural variable.

## Teleoperation & Data Collection

**[Reverse to Advance: Teleoperation-Cost Effective Hard Policy Learning from Reversed Easy Tasks](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13455)**
Exploits directional asymmetry: reversing easy tasks generates supervision for hard tasks. Closed-loop pipeline with hierarchical data refinement and iterative policy learning improves hard-task success rates without extensive teleoperation.

**[Worlds in One Demo: A Synthetic Data Engine for Learning Open-World Mobile Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13154)**
WANDA reconstructs Gaussian splats and interaction trajectories from one RGBD demo, then rearranges segments with whole-body motion planning. Supports cross-embodiment and cross-environment generalization from a single real demonstration.

**[PhysClaw-0: A Symbiotic Agentic System for Robot Autonomy via Language Corrections](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14047)**
Retains and reuses corrections across sessions via Corrective Memory. Matches teleoperation success while reducing human working time to 16%. Fine-tuned policies match teleoperation-trained success at fraction of cost.

## Sim-to-Real & Simulation

**[GPUSimBench: Towards Scalable and Reliable GPU-Accelerated Simulators in Embodied AI](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13059)**
Benchmarks physical consistency, scalability, and non-determinism in GPU simulators (Isaac Lab, Genesis). Quantifies run-to-run variability and identifies four regimes of stochasticity.

**[Learning Physics-Guided Residual Dynamics for Deformable Object Simulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13451)**
PGRD combines spring-mass simulator with learned residual corrections via sliding-window transformer. Enables MPC-based manipulation planning and action-conditioned video prediction via 3D Gaussian Splatting.

## In-Hand Manipulation & Grasping

**[Towards a Modular Bin-picking Framework for Handling Object Pose Uncertainties](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13698)**
Modular framework handling both pose estimation uncertainty and grasping errors. Uses pose distribution estimation, second-viewpoint fusion, and grasping error compensation modules.

## Human-Robot Interaction

**[HRIBench: Benchmarking Interaction-Centric Human-Robot Collaboration](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13056)**
Diagnostic benchmark with 13 role-conditioned tasks (Instructor, Collaborator, Intruder). Evaluates GR00T, pi0.5, ACT; shows current VLA policies struggle with temporal coordination and intent-aware behavior.

**[Legible Shared Autonomy: Implicit Communication of Robot Belief through Motion](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.29846)**
Makes robot's inferred goals legible through motion, enabling users to understand robot beliefs. Confidence-aware adaptive authority allocation improves user understanding and reduces control effort.

## Object Goal Navigation

**[HRO: Hierarchical Room-to-Object Framework for Zero-Shot Object Goal Navigation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13072)**
LLM-driven hierarchical navigation: coarse room-level reasoning then fine object localization. Outperforms flat LLM methods on Gibson and HM3D.

## Humanoid & Legged Locomotion

**[EgoHTR: Egocentric 4D Demonstrations of Human Terrain Traversal](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13472)**
55 scene-aligned 4D human motion sequences in complex environments using egocentric wearables. Demonstrates hardware deployment on Unitree G1 for perceptive locomotion policies.

**[Agile perceptive multi-skill locomotion for quadrupedal robots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13579)**
APT-RL enables multi-skill locomotion with autonomous transitions using only onboard perception. Achieves 6 m/s peak speed, traversing stairs, hurdles, stepping stones with a single policy.

## World Models & Planning

**[GigaWorld-Policy-0.5: A Faster and Stronger WAM](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13960)**
Action-centered World Action Model using Mixture-of-Transformers. Achieves 85 ms inference on RTX 4090 by separating visual dynamics and action generation.

**[Warp RL: Reshaping Base Policy Distributions for Dynamics Adaptation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.31043)**
Replaces additive residual correction with invertible state-conditioned transformations using rational-quadratic spline flows. Outperforms residual RL under dynamics shift, with 30% faster task completion on real peg-insertion.

## Perception & State Estimation

**[Deformable State Estimation for Autonomous Surgical Tissue Retraction Under Partial Observability](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13475)**
Learned estimator reconstructs full deformable mesh from 40 noisy vertex observations using PCA latent + MLP. Achieves 98.1% of oracle performance in multi-step retraction.

**[Topology-Agnostic Mesh Reconstruction of Deformable Objects from Sparse Touch](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13479)**
Single estimator handles rope, cloth, and volumetric bodies from sparse touches only. Reduces reconstruction error ~2/3 vs baselines. Active touch selection via deep-ensemble uncertainty further improves results.

## Navigation & Autonomous Driving

**[A 3DGS-Driven Dynamic Viewpoint and Vibrotactile Framework for Subsea Teleoperation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13067)**
Combines 3D Gaussian Splatting exocentric visualization with torso-mounted vibrotactile suit for underwater ROV teleoperation. fNIRS study shows 3DGS view sustains executive control under latency.

**[Ego-Dynamics-Augmented World Model for Autonomous Driving](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.13410)**
DynaDreamer augments world models with explicit ego-dynamics prior, improving task success by 28-61% and enabling zero-shot cross-chassis adaptation.

Decentralized repair policy inspired by biological wound healing. Gathers ~80% of surviving modules despite 30% failure in PyBullet simulation.

Novel actuator geometry encircling the limb for lower-limb exoskeletons. Achieves 7.5 Nm/kg continuous torque density with 894 g mass.
