### **Large-Scale Data & Foundation Models for Manipulation**

*   **[6] CAIP: Contrastive Action-Image Pre-training for Visuomotor Control** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17256))
    *   Proposes a vision encoder pretrained by contrasting images with 3D hand keypoints from egocentric human video. This "action-centric" pretraining outperforms models like DINOv2 and R3M on dexterous manipulation tasks, showing that human hand poses serve as a scalable proxy for robot actions.
*   **[5] ACE-Ego-0: Unifying Egocentric Human and Robotic Data for VLA Pretraining** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17200))
    *   Presents a unified VLA pretraining framework using both robot data and a scalable pipeline that converts egocentric human videos into pseudo-action trajectories. Uses a reliability-aware weighting scheme to handle noisy human labels, demonstrating strong transfer to real-world bimanual tasks.
*   **[30] Qwen-RobotManip Technical Report** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17846))
    *   A Vision-Language-Action (VLA) foundation model that aligns heterogeneous manipulation data across representation, motion, and behavioral dimensions. By using a large-scale pretraining corpus (~38,100 hours) from open-source data and human videos, it achieves emergent generalization like zero-shot instruction following and cross-embodiment transfer.

### **Dexterous & Anthropomorphic Hand Design**

*   **[15] DexLink Hand: A Compact, Affordable, 16-DOF Linkage-Driven Hand** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17418))
    *   Introduces a highly dexterous, linkage-driven anthropomorphic hand with 16 independent actuators, weighing only 320g at a cost under $400. It achieves a max Kapandji score and reproduces all 33 Feix grasp types, demonstrating a promising platform for dexterous manipulation and teleoperation.

    *   A framework that converts raw 3D assets into "manipulation-ready" versions by automatically generating labels like grasp poses and articulation waypoints using VLM reasoning and physics simulation. Supports downstream tasks like affordance detection and data collection for diverse embodiments.
*   **[21] GASE: Gaussian Splatting-Based Automated System for Reconstructing Embodied-Simulation Environments** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17520))
    *   A highly automated system for building simulation environments from real-world scans. It uses panoramic cameras for rapid scanning and a 3D Gaussian Splatting pipeline to reconstruct scenes and objects, achieving a sim-to-real performance gap of less than 10% on real-robot tasks.

### **Tactile Sensing & Multimodal Sensing**

*   **[23] MuseVLA: An Adaptive Multimodal Sensing Vision-Language-Action Model** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17598))
    *   Integrates novel sensors (e.g., temperature, audio) into a VLA model as on-demand tools. It generates a "sensor token" to decide which modality to use and converts sensor readings into a unified image representation for action generation. Achieves an 80.6% success rate on challenging dexterous tasks requiring multimodal input.

### **Visuomotor Policies, Action Generation & Planning**

*   **[14] LeaP: A Learnable Source Prior for Generative Robot Policies** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17408))
    *   Replaces the standard Gaussian noise used to initialize generative models (like diffusion) with a learned, proprioception-conditioned prior. This improves action generation by providing an observation-informed starting point, achieving an 81.6% average success rate on 15 RoboTwin tasks.
*   **[35] LAGO Policy: Latency-Aware Asynchronous Diffusion Policies** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17982))
    *   Addresses motion discontinuity and collisions in asynchronous diffusion policies by integrating trajectory optimization. It uses latency-aware guidance and goal-directed planning to produce smooth, collision-free motion for real-world manipulation.

### **Teleoperation & Human-to-Robot Transfer**

*   **[11] EgoInfinity: A Web-Scale 4D Hand-Object Interaction Data Engine** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17385))
    *   A universal data engine that converts internet videos of hand-object interaction into executable robot trajectories. It reconstructs 4D hand-object states from arbitrary RGB video and retargets the motion to diverse robot morphologies, enabling video-to-action robot learning at scale.
*   **[58] OmniRetarget: Interaction-Preserving Data Generation for Humanoid Loco-Manipulation** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.26633))
    *   An interaction-preserving retargeting engine for humanoid robots. It uses an interaction mesh to preserve critical spatial/contact relationships during motion retargeting, enabling RL policies for complex loco-manipulation and parkour from captured human data.

### **Benchmarks & Diagnostics**

*   **[42] EBench: Elemental Diagnosis of Generalist Mobile Manipulation Policies** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18239))
    *   A diagnostic benchmark for mobile manipulation that goes beyond single success rates. It profiles policies across 5 capability and 4 generalization dimensions, revealing distinct strengths and weaknesses in models like π0.5 and InternVLA that a single score would hide.
*   **[36] Uncertainty Quantification for Flow-Based VLAs** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.18043))
    *   Derives a method for quantifying epistemic uncertainty in flow-matching VLAs by measuring velocity-field disagreement. This uncertainty estimate is used for failure detection and to guide active fine-tuning, reducing the number of required demonstrations by at least 22%.

### **Reinforcement Learning & Skill Acquisition**

*   **[31] WAM-RL: World-Action Model RL with Reconstruction Rewards** ([link](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.17906))
    *   Introduces an RL framework for joint online optimization of a world model and an action model. A key finding is that jointly training both is critical for long-horizon tasks, while optimizing the actor alone only helps short-horizon tasks.
