### Dexterous Manipulation & Multi-Finger Grasping

- **[TacRefineNet: Goal-Conditioned Tactile Grasp Refinement for Edge-Prominent Objects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2509.25746)**: Proposes a tactile-only framework for local grasp refinement. Uses a Siamese policy network to predict corrective wrist poses from current and target tactile images, forming a tactile servoing loop. Demonstrates zero-shot sim-to-real transfer on an 11-DoF hand for thin objects like plates and rods.

- **[Scaling Cross-Embodiment World Models for Dexterous Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.01177)**: Represents human and robot hands as sets of 3D particles and actions as displacement fields, creating a shared geometric space for learning across diverse morphologies. A graph-based world model trained on this representation enables effective model-predictive control on new robotic hands.

- **[Design and stability analysis of an underactuated hand with passively rotating fingers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18950)**: Presents an underactuated robotic hand design with a revolute joint at the finger base for passive spatial rotation, enabling both cylindrical and spherical grasps. Validated on a fully mechanical prototype.

- **[ChronoFlow-Policy: Unifying Past-Current-Future Interaction Flow in Visuomotor Policy Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.31493)**: Introduces ChronoFlow, a unified temporal representation of past, current, and future interaction dynamics using sparse 3D keypoints of objects and grippers. A diffusion-based policy trained with this representation shows improved performance on long-horizon manipulation tasks.

- **[EquiBim: Learning Symmetry-Equivariant Policy for Bimanual Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08541)**: Introduces a symmetry-equivariant policy learning framework that enforces bilateral equivariance between observations and actions during training, improving robustness in bimanual manipulation.

### Tactile Sensing & Visuomotor Policies

- **[MVP-Tac: A Miniaturized Dual-Modal Vision and Photoelastic Tactile Sensor for RMIS](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18660)**: A compact vision-based tactile sensor using reflective photoelastic imaging. Provides co-located vision and tactile sensing for robot-assisted surgery, achieving high accuracy in hardness classification of tumors.

- **[TransDex: Pre-training Visuo-Tactile Policy with Point Cloud Reconstruction for Dexterous Manipulation of Transparent Objects](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.13869)**: Uses self-supervised point cloud reconstruction pre-training to recover the 3D structure of transparent objects. A 3D visuo-tactile fusion motor policy built on this pre-training outperforms baselines in real-world transparent object manipulation.

- **[ImplicitRDP: An End-to-End Visual-Force Diffusion Policy with Structural Slow-Fast Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2512.10946)**: Integrates visual planning and reactive force control in a single diffusion network using a Structural Slow-Fast Learning mechanism. Designed to handle asynchronicity between slow visual context and fast force dynamics for contact-rich manipulation.

- **[CONTACT: CONtact-aware TACTile Learning for Robotic Disassembly](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.08560)**: Systematically investigates the role of tactile sensing in contact-rich disassembly tasks. Finds that structured force-field representations (TacFF) significantly improve success rates over vision-only or TacRGB baselines.

- **[Fabric Pneumatic Artificial Muscles Based on the Drawstring Principle](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18641)**: Proposes a novel pneumatic artificial muscle that eliminates radial deformation during contraction. Demonstrates high load capacity and power density, with applications in bionic robots.

### Humanoid & Legged Locomotion (Focus on Manipulation)

- **[FARO: Feasibility-Aware Robot Motion Optimization](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18362)**: Proposes a nested kino-dynamic framework for rapid feasibility checking and trajectory generation for humanoid loco-manipulation, integrated with an LLM-based contact plan sampling strategy.

- **[VOFA: Visual Object Goal Pushing with Force-Adaptive Control for Humanoids](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.01518)**: A two-level hierarchical architecture for humanoids to push large objects to arbitrary goals using onboard vision. Employs a visuomotor policy for commands and a force-adaptive whole-body controller for robustness.

### Teleoperation & Sim-to-Real

- **[SimFoundry: Modular and Automated Scene Generation for Policy Learning and Evaluation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.28276)**: An automated system for zero-shot real-to-sim scene construction from video, enabling the generation of diverse digital cousins for policy training. Shows strong correlation between simulation and real-world performance across multiple manipulation tasks.

- **[RAPT: Model-Predictive Out-of-Distribution Detection and Failure Diagnosis for Sim-to-Real Humanoid Deployment](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.01515)**: A lightweight, self-supervised deployment monitor for Sim-to-Real transfer that detects OOD states and diagnoses failure causes, improving safety for learned control policies.

- **[Two-Stage Extrinsic Calibration of a Static Line-Scanning Lidar with a Rotary Platform](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18578)**: Proposes automated methods for calibrating a line-scanning lidar on a rotary platform, crucial for generating accurate 3D point clouds for precise object scanning and inspection.

### Robot Planning & World Models

- **[Masked Visual Actions for Unified World Modeling](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19343)**: Expresses action as a partially revealed trajectory in a video, enabling a single video model to act as a forward dynamics model and support inverse modeling. Finetuned with only 15 hours of data.

- **[STeP: Signal Temporal Logic for Precise Specifications for Action Generation with Vision Language Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18580)**: A hierarchical framework using Signal Temporal Logic (STL) as a shared representation to improve precision, reliability, and interpretability of language-conditioned robot planning.

- **[Agentic Real2Sim: Physics-based World Modeling with Vision-Language Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.19190)**: A framework using VL agents to convert real-world object-robot interaction recordings into simulatable digital twins for policy learning.

- **[WorldScape Policy 2.0: Empowering Steerable World Action Modeling with Reasoning-Augmented Memory](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18840)**: Introduces reasoning-augmented long short-term memory for World Action Models, improving long-horizon autonomous planning and fine-grained instruction following in manipulation.

### LLM Agents for Robotics & Embodied AI

- **[RoboInspector: Unveiling the Unreliability of Policy Code for LLM-enabled Robotic Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2508.21378)**: Systematically analyzes unreliability in LLM-generated policy code for robotic manipulation, identifying four main failure behaviors and introducing a refinement approach.

- **[Guide, Think, Act: Interactive Embodied Reasoning in Vision-Language-Action Models](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.13632)**: Proposes an interactive VLA framework (GTA-VLA) that allows users to guide robot policies with explicit visual cues (e.g., affordance points), achieving state-of-the-art success rates on in-domain and OOD tasks.

- **[LifelongVLA: Towards Human-like Physical Intelligence](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.14852)**: A lifelong learning framework for VLA models that addresses the plasticity-stability trade-off with a dual-timescale LoRA gating module, enabling efficient skill expansion without retraining.

- **[AnchorRefine: Synergy-Manipulation Based on Trajectory Anchor and Residual Refinement for VLAs](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.17787)**: A hierarchical framework that factorizes VLA action modeling into coarse trajectory anchors and residual refinement, improving precision in manipulation tasks.
