### Vision-Language-Action (VLA) Models & Policy Learning

*   **[`2606.13856`] Output-Level Regularization Eliminates the Seed Lottery in Single-GPU VLA Fine-Tuning** (Sam et al.) - Identifies a "seed lottery" in VLA fine-tuning where a single seed can cause catastrophic failure. The cause is "output collapse," which is solved by using regularizers like VICReg instead of weight-level methods.
*   **[`2606.13886`] PhysVLA: Towards Physically-Grounded VLA for Embodied Robotic Manipulation** (Chandra et al.) - Introduces a plug-and-play inference-time framework that corrects VLA actions for physical consistency (Euler-Lagrange dynamics). It improves success rates without retraining.
*   **[`2606.14255`] ReactVLA: Fast and Lightweight Reactive Robot Manipulation via Improved Mean Flow Action Generation** (Guo et al.) - A lightweight VLA framework using an "Improved Mean Flow" (iMF) action generator to reduce sampling steps, resulting in faster, real-time reactive control.
*   **[`2606.14084`] Self-Improving VLA Policies: Selected Diffusion Noise for Spurious-Robust Action Smoothing** (Nguyen et al.) - A training-free method (SDN) that selects noise vectors for diffusion-based VLA policies, improving robustness and action smoothness by avoiding reliance on spurious correlations.
*   **[`2606.14238`] When and How Severely: Scenario-Specific Safety Envelopes for Driving VLAs** (Priyadershi & Frtunikj) - Evaluates a driving VLA and finds that a single aggregate safety threshold is insufficient; a two-dimensional safety envelope is needed to capture scenario-specific failure severity.
*   **[`2606.14375`] Elastic Queries Reinforcement Learning: Self-Aware Policy Execution for VLA Models** (Wang et al.) - Introduces a method (EQRL) that makes VLA inference "elastic" by adaptively adjusting the denoising budget and action chunk length based on task difficulty.
*   **[`2604.21391`] From Noise to Intent: Anchoring Generative VLA Policies with Residual Bridges** (Zhong et al.) - Proposes ResVLA, which separates robot control into a deterministic "intent" and a stochastic "residual," anchoring generation on the intent for faster convergence and robustness.
*   **[`2606.14048`] WAM4D: Fast 4D World Action Model via Spatial Register Tokens** (Li et al.) - A fast world-action model that uses lightweight "spatial register tokens" to transfer geometric priors into a video-action transformer, enabling 4D reasoning without costly decoding.
*   **[`2605.27284`] FineVLA: Fine-Grained Instruction Alignment for Steerable Vision-Language-Action Policies** (Hu et al.) - Presents a framework for fine-grained VLA supervision, creating a dataset of 47K trajectories with detailed execution instructions (e.g., "use left arm," "approach from the top") to improve policy steerability.
*   **[`2605.22183`] Action with Visual Primitives** (Guo et al.) - Proposes AVP, where a VLM emits "visual-primitive tokens" (e.g., target region) to condition a flow-matching action expert, decoupling high-level intent from low-level control.
*   **[`2606.08555`] FAWAM: Force-Aware World Action Models for Closed-Loop Contact-Rich Manipulation** (He et al.) - Integrates force/torque signals into a world-action model at three levels: perception, prediction, and closed-loop execution, leading to significant success rate improvements.
*   **[`2606.15768`] LaWAM: Latent World Action Models for Efficient Dynamics-Aware Robot Policies** (Chen et al.) - Proposes LaWAM, which uses predicted latent visual subgoals to make robot control dynamics-aware, achieving low-latency inference superior to pixel-space world models.
*   **[`2606.16286`] FlowMPC: Improving Flow Matching policies with World Models** (Hamel) - Combines an imitation-learned Flow Matching policy with a learned world model (TD-MPC2) for test-time planning (MPPI), improving performance on manipulation tasks.
*   **[`2606.14665`] EgoGuide: Egocentric Guidance for Efficient Robot-Free Demonstration Collection and Learning** (Xu et al.) - Introduces a robot-free data collection interface with online quality guidance (EgoGuide) and a residual policy robust to viewpoint-varying egocentric cameras.
*   **[`2606.14535`] Spatially Conditioned Diffusion Policy: Learning Precise and Robust Manipulation with a Single RGB Camera** (Kim et al.) - Proposes SCDP, a diffusion policy using spatial conditioning that samples features along intermediate trajectories for precise single-camera manipulation.
*   **[`2605.03065`] OGPO: Sample Efficient Full-Finetuning of Generative Control Policies** (Patil et al.) - Introduces OGPO, a sample-efficient algorithm for finetuning diffusion/flow-based policies using off-policy critics and a modified PPO objective.
*   **[`2606.16253`] Learned Image Compression for Vision-Language-Action Models** (Kim et al.) - Proposes Spatially Adaptive Rate Control (SPARC) for learned image compression tailored to VLA tasks, significantly improving the bitrate-success tradeoff in teleoperation.
*   **[`2606.15260`] Trust-Region Diffusion Policies for Massively Parallel On-Policy RL** (Le et al.) - Introduces TruDi, which enables training diffusion policies with on-policy RL in massively parallel simulations by enforcing a trust-region constraint.
*   **[`2606.14561`] ORCA: A Platform for Open-Source Dexterity Research** (Capuano et al.) - An open-source research stack for dexterous manipulation, unifying simulation, teleoperation (VR), and retargeting for hands like the LEAP Hand.

### Dexterous Manipulation & Robotic Hands

*   **[`2606.14250`] SyLink Hand: A Synergy-Inspired Linkage-Driven Anthropomorphic Hand for Human-Like Dexterity** (Wu et al.) - Presents an anthropomorphic hand (19 joints, 11 actuators) using a novel spherical four-bar linkage for decoupled motion, achieving human-like kinematics and low cost (~$400).
*   **[`2606.14606`] Impedance MPC with Disturbance Estimation for Dexterous Hand Control** (Cao) - An actuator-agnostic Impedance MPC framework for dexterous fingers that runs at 500Hz, enforcing hard constraints on contact force and jerk.
*   **[`2606.12728`] EquiDexFlow: Contact-Grounded SE(3)-Equivariant Dexterous Grasp Generative Flows** (Enwerem et al.) - A flow-matching model that jointly predicts wrist pose, joint angles, and contact forces, ensuring SE(3) equivariance and friction cone compliance.
*   **[`2606.14551`] TRACE: Trajectory-Routed Causal Memory for Delayed-Evidence Visuomotor Imitation** (Li et al.) - A memory framework that uses "path signatures" of the robot's trajectory to store and retrieve crucial visual evidence needed for tasks where early visual cues disappear before decision time.

### Tactile Sensing

*   **[`2606.13746`] Scalable Dynamic Tactile Sensing Enabled by Passive and Flexible Acoustic Waveguides** (Long et al.) - Reports a passive, flexible tactile sensing paradigm using acoustic waveguides. It enables large-area, scalable arrays for applications like tactile gloves and skins.
*   **[`2606.13877`] ContactWorld: What Matters in Vision-Tactile World Models for Contact-Rich Manipulation** (Zhang et al.) - A benchmark and study finding that spatially structured (point clouds) and temporally continuous representations are best for planning. Tactile force-fields improve performance when combined with point clouds.
*   **[`2606.14981`] Inference-time Policy Steering via Vision and Touch** (Wu et al.) - Proposes ViTaL, a visuo-tactile inference-time steering framework. It uses a visual verifier for mode selection and a tactile-guided diffusion editor for refining contact-rich actions.

*   **[`2606.14218`] Universal Manipulation Exoskeleton: Learning Compliant Whole-body Policies with Real-time Torque Feedback** (Liang et al.) - A low-cost, portable upper-limb exoskeleton (UME) providing real-time torque feedback and haptics, enabling learning of compliant, whole-body policies.
